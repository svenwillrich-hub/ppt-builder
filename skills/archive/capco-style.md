# Capco Consulting Style

## Design Identity
- Teal accent color as primary (#008B8B or similar teal)
- Century Gothic or similar geometric sans-serif typography
- High-density content grids — maximize information per slide
- No section divider slides — rhythm comes from consistent headers

## Layout Rules
- Header: Numbered section badge + title on every slide
- Content area: Full-width, multi-column grids (2-3 columns)
- Footer: Thin line with page number and confidentiality notice
- Use teal accent bars and highlights sparingly

## Content Density
- More content per slide than typical consulting decks
- Use tables, comparison grids, and capability matrices
- Icon grids for capability overviews
- Progress indicators and status dashboards

## Visual Components
- KPI widgets with donut charts
- RAG status indicators (Red/Amber/Green)
- Timeline bars with milestones
- Comparison tables with checkmarks
